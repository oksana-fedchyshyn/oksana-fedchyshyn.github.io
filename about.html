OKSANA FEDCHYSHYN

Oksana was born in 1999 in Lviv, Ukraine.

EDUCATION

In 2014, she joined the Lviv State College of Decorative and Applied Arts named by I. Trush, where she studied at the Decorative Painting department.

In 2018 she became a student of the Lviv National Academy of Arts, the Department of Graphic Design, where she continues to study now.

AWARDS AND SPECIAL PREMIUMS

2018 - Laureate of the Mariusz Kazan premium.
